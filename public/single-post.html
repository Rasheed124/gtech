Key Takeaways from State and Future of Crypto, Web3, and DeFi in Nigeria

10th Oct 2022

We’re thrilled to bring you the insightful revelations from Furex’s Founder and CEO, Fure Eviosekwofa, who took center stage at the Lagos Tech Fest. Engaging with fellow Crypto stakeholders, he delved deep into the State and Future of Crypto, Web3, and DeFi in Nigeria, unveiling key takeaways that promise to shape the nation’s financial landscape.

1. Championing Financial Inclusion:

Fure underscored the transformative power of crypto in driving financial inclusion. He illuminated the critical need for widespread access, emphasizing crypto’s potential to shield against currency devaluation—a poignant concern for the underbanked. In doing so, he painted a vision of a more inclusive financial future, where crypto becomes a powerful tool for economic empowerment.
